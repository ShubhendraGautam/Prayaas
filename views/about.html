What We Do?

A social activity to brighten the future.

Education

We provide free and equal education to all

Donation Drive

Donations are made to encourage children for school

Awareness programs

Various awareness programs like held to teach moral and social lessons

Workshops

Various fun activities are held throught the year.

College visit

College visits are regularly held for special occasions.

Cultural Programs

On auspicious events like republic day, holi, diwali various events are held

Social Initiative and Society Services(SISS)


SISS is an add-on course that has been introduced in the academic session and is associated with PRAYAAS. It consists of 5 phases:

Introduction

An introductory phase where a student gets to interact with the children

Village life

This phase deals with village visits, donation drives and workshops

Social Ed

This phase deals with the core of Prayaas, which is educating the kids.

Moral Ed

This deals with various workshops & sessions where we tell them how to identify the ethical aspect of things.

Leadership

This deals with management of Prayaas kids, classes, events and the team.
